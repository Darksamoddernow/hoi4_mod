House Rules - Historical Session
General Rules Section:
1) No mass releasing puppets; limited to one per year (Exception: Japan may release Cambodia and Laos for a player Siam to take and form their formable nation).
2) No boosting party popularity or staging coop unless agreed by everyone.
3) No memes and exploits that will in general be thought of as abuse and have players leave. Use common sense. Examples: 
3.1) Building up planning bonus against a friendly frontline. 
3.2) Destroying your own buildings by increasing resistance in anticipation of an enemy attack.
4) No unhistorical ideology switches unless agreed upon by everyone. (Alternative War) / (Non Historical) apart from designated countries that can switch.
5) NO military or Docking access unless in faction together.
5.1) Poland is allowed to ask the Soviet Union for military access upon completing the focus "Polish Armed Forces in the East". Poland is not allowed to call in the Allies vs the Soviet Union unless agreed by everyone.
6) No manual war justification before January 1st 1939.
7) Air controller nation rule: no more than half of your produced aircraft can be sent to faction members or by lend-lease.
8) Cannot release puppets in an active warzone unless triggered by event or the setup of a decision.
9) Subjects must go same ideology path as overlord, must be called to war. Faction members must be called to war unless they have entry restriction date.
10) Nations MUST! play historical IN GENERAL UP TO MID 1942) (Unless part of specific list below).
11) No deleting buildings.
12) Even on historical settings some nations are allowed to switch ideology, examples: Hungary, Romania, Bulgaria, Finland, Iraq, Siam and Sweden can switch to fascism.
13) Spain cannot join the axis or actively wage war before December 1941, Spain has to go fascist unless the match is classified as non historical.
14) The Following Nations can be attacked regardless of the 1942 historical play rule: Tibet and Sinkiang.
15) Players to take part in the MP must be able to hold at least 2 speed from the start of Barbarossa, failure to sustain such speed after two re-hosts will result in the player being asked to leave the match or be forcibly removed from it, this is a necessary measure due to the increase in player lag and input lag rendering MP's unplayable and unsustainable.

Spanish Civil War Section:
1) Only axis & Soviet can send attaches and equipment to the Spanish Civil War.
2) No sending tanks to the Spanish civil war. (It will make the war end to fast).
3) Spain cannot join a faction during the civil war.

Sino-Jap War Section:
1) Attaches & Equipment can only be sent to Nat-China by USA and Allies members. Soviet can send to PRC. No other nations can send attaches & Equipment. (Tanks cannot be sent as lend lease).
2) Japan must puppet or declare war on China before January 1st 1938.
3) Neither the Axis nor The Allies or the Commintern can send volunteers to intervene in the Sino-Japanese war

Air Rules Section:
1) No airwing size below 100 unless it is air volunteers, carriers, or less than 200 planes of that type in total.
2) Strategic bomber wings cannot have their target region switched more often than every 14 days.
3) Port Strike mission follows the same rule for Strategic Bombing listed in the rule above.

Navy Rules Section:
1) You are not allowed to "Pre Lay Mines" Anywhere to hinder a nation that you are not in a war with.
Example. = Laying Mines around China, Japan and/or Philippines as UK, BECAUSE you know there will be a war in a year time, is NOT ALLOWED.
2) No Death stacks:
-Example: 1 task force needs to be at max 12 capitals (CV/BB/SBB/CA) and max 48 Screens (CLs/DDs), this rule is also implemented into the game balance so death stacking will bring your fleet penalties in combat.
3) When you are no longer at war you need to stop mine-laying missions, that they are not auto-stopped is a bug.
